about me

Hello again! My name is Mohit Patel. I am currently a Computer Science final year student and a self-taught full-stack developer. I draw pleasure from solving people’s problems and passionate about building excellent Websites that improves the lives of those around me. I love spending time on fixing little details and optimizing application (or web app). Also, I like working in a team, you’ll learn faster and much more.

I have joined a private college because of a decent rank in the entrance exam. After a while, I started losing interest in what was taught is classes and it wasn’t my cup of tea. Meanwhile, I started competitive programming, web development, exploring technologies, etc. In the second year, I joined ZeitLabs as a Frontend Developer Intern which helped me build my basics. The projects I did here plus the concept, helped my problem-solving skills, and also got a taste of how things work in the real world.
